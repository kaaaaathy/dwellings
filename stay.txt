In some gradation 
of figuration 
move from one axis to the other.  
A mourning dove figures heavily 
in the work.  Sweet childhood 
flowers here,  alongside 
inert superposition 
of a few pronouns.  
Creosote never appears psychically 
but I miss the longroad 
the Desert music. 
Roll onwards to a tipsy slang, 
Evanesce with the lights 
and vague social memory 
Scared to google and change mind  
Afterall, certain things are seamed 
and Government identification 
is hardly romantic.
