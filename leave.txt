The movie theater is in a movie theater 
is it a Jungian space?  The picture I’m watching 
has never happened.  
Terrifyingly, there are classrooms of young children.  
There are storefronts of children's clothes.  
I want to buy a blue dress, a teenager’s dress,  
In the fitting room I feel shame.  
A security guard yells at me and I dread the spring.  
In numerology, forty four is a stable number,  
a protective one.  
This makes sense.  I have four wheel drive. 
On the ride home, I see a black cat.  
A white dog. A deer lapsing into brush.  
Make a tepid wish. A cocoon made of jade.  
A table made of threes.  
What happens if you turn the three on its side?  
Something profane about calendars  
and unforgettable.
